Follow-up DSS Study - Music Preference Survey

Welcome to the Follow-up Study

Thank you for participating in our initial survey! As part of the DSS (Decision Support Systems) course, we (Group 2 students) have selected a set of songs based on your music preferences. This follow-up survey is conducted by students under the supervision of dr.ir. Martijn Willemsen.

This follow-up survey will take approximately 10-15 minutes. You will be asked to evaluate a selection of songs and provide your feedback on them. Your honest feedback will help us understand music preferences better.

Please note: We are asking for your consent again because this follow-up study involves collecting new data (your evaluations and ratings of the recommendations). Your participation in this follow-up is voluntary and independent from the initial survey.

Privacy and Data Use

  • Participation is voluntary and you may stop at any time without consequences.
  • Your personal data will not be shared outside the research team and will be deleted after the study.
  • All responses are stored securely on TU/e servers and analysed anonymously by the students.
  • Results will only be reported at a group level and may be used in academic assignments and presentations.
